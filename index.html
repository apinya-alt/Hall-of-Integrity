A Civic Awareness Game

Hall of Integrity

Walk through a government building. Tap any character to investigate. Spot all ten violations of good governance before the clock runs out.

How to play

  1. Tap any character to read what they are saying and doing.
  2. Deliver your verdict: violation, legitimate, or decide later.
  3. Catching a violation circles them in red. Ten violations are hidden in the building.
  4. Three staff are doing their jobs properly. Verifying good practice earns smaller points.
  5. Careless judgement costs points. A false accusation against an innocent staffer is the heaviest penalty.
